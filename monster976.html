Ducati MONSTER 976 PLUS Overview
A modern take on the Monster that highlights the personality of the bike and maximises enjoyment for the rider. The new Iceberg White colourway further emphasises its contemporary style and combination of elegant and sports-oriented details.
Narrow sides and a front frame that ends at the height of the vertical cylinder head ensure the rider can easily touch the ground with their feet when stationary. The muscular bison-back tank for which the Monster stands out is reproportioned, now more compact to ensure more comfortable and effective ergonomics.
A design that is all about simple, clean lines. An engine, a saddle, a tank and a handlebar. Everything you need to ride and have fun, nothing else.
The Monster mounts the Euro 5 approved 937 cc Testastretta 11° engine with desmodromic timing.
