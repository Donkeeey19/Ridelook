HARLEY DAVIDSON FAT BOB Overview
The all-new Fat Bob® is 21.9lb lighter with Tarmac-eating traction delivered by Milwaukee-Eight® engine power. Updated suspension and the reinvented Softail® frame smooths out the ride and provides agile cornering.
The all-new Fat Bob features aggressive, unapologetic styling, mind-blowing performance and handling, premium finishes, and your choice of the powerful Milwaukee-Eight® 107 or 114 Big Twin Engine.
The most powerful, agile, and responsive cruisers in Harley-Davidson history feature a lighter and stiffer frame, high-performance suspension and the smooth, unrelenting torque of a fully counter-balanced Milwaukee-Eight® V-Twin engine.
Interpreting Harley-Davidson’s history, authenticity and styling DNA through a modern lens, the eight new Softail models feature all-new designs that strongly differentiate them from their predecessors and each other. From a new base of style, comfort and performance, the next generation of factory custom cruisers are ready for riders to take even higher with their own personalization.
